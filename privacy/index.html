Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Last updated: March 2026

DeadHangs.com ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at deadhangs.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it and the choices available to you. By using this site you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We do not require account creation or collect personal information such as your name, email address or phone number through standard site usage. The data we gather falls into two categories.

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit DeadHangs.com, our analytics and advertising partners may collect technical data including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages viewed, time spent on each page and general geographic location. This information is collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies. It helps us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve content and user experience.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use first-party cookies for basic site functionality and third-party cookies set by our analytics and advertising partners. Most browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences through their settings. You can disable cookies entirely, though this may affect site functionality.

Analytics

We use web analytics services to measure traffic and usage patterns. These services collect aggregated, anonymised data that helps us identify which content is most useful to our readers and where we can improve. Analytics data is not used to identify individual visitors.

Display Advertising

DeadHangs.com uses third-party advertising platforms, which may include Ezoic and Google AdSense, to display advertisements. These services use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your browsing history across the web. You can opt out of personalised advertising through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page or your browser's privacy settings.

Amazon Associates Disclosure

DeadHangs.com is a participant in the Amazon Associates Programme, an affiliate advertising programme designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by linking to Amazon.co.uk and affiliated sites. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence our editorial content or product recommendations.

How We Use Your Information

The data collected through analytics and advertising is used to:

  • Analyse site traffic and user behaviour to improve content quality
  • Serve relevant advertisements to support the cost of operating this site
  • Monitor site performance and diagnose technical issues
  • Understand which topics and training guides are most valuable to readers

Data Sharing and Sales

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared with our analytics and advertising partners strictly for the purposes described in this policy. We do not transfer personal data to any party for marketing purposes unrelated to this site.

Data Retention and Security

Analytics data is retained according to the default retention periods set by our analytics providers. We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through this site, though no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

DeadHangs.com is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided personal data through this site, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct or delete personal data associated with your visit. You may also opt out of personalised advertising and analytics tracking using your browser settings or the opt-out tools referenced above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at hello@deadhangs.com.
